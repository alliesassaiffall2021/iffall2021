Art for All, and All of Art
Ocean Tactile Painting, Create and Enjoy!

This Inclusive tactile art is aimed towards 4-7 year olds with vision impairments or blindess, however, this project is engaging and fun for all children. The tactile materials, such as puffy paint, cotton balls, and foam stickers, aim to create sensorimotor engagements for children with vision imnpairments or blindness, but these features are fun and provide enrichment for all children.
NOTE: This project involves an adult to set up and facilitate the project

The Supplies

  • Foam Sea Animal Stickers
  • Blue and Green Puffy Paint (1 Part Elmers Glue, 1 Part Paint)
  • Scissors
  • Blue Construction Paper
  • Glue Sticks
  • Sticky Ahesive Paper

The Steps

  • 1. (ADULT) CUT STICKY PAPER TO THE SIZE OF SAND BAR AND STICK TO BLUE PAPER.
  • 2. (CHILD) PLACE SAND AND SHELLS ON STICKY PAPER
  • 3. (CHILD) PLACE PUFFY PAINT IN THE CENTER OF THE BLUE PAPER TO CREATE OCEAN
  • 4. (CHILD) USE GLUE STICK TO ATTACH COTTON BALLS TO THE SKY
  • 5. (CHILD) PLACE FOAM STICKERS ON PUFFY PAINT OCEAN. ALLOW PAINT TO DRY, THEN ENJOY THE ART!
